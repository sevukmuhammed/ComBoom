Privacy Policy

ComBoom — M3Studios · Last updated: February 12, 2026

1. Introduction

M3Studios ("we", "our", "us") built ComBoom as a free, ad-supported mobile game. This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use ComBoom and how it is used.

2. Information We Collect

ComBoom does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play. The following data may be collected through third-party services integrated into the app:

Google Play Games Services (Android)

  • Your Google Play Games display name
  • Your game scores submitted to leaderboards

Apple Game Center (iOS)

  • Your Game Center display name
  • Your game scores submitted to leaderboards

Advertising (Google AdMob)

  • Device advertising identifier
  • General device information (device model, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

3. How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Display your scores on public leaderboards
  • Show your display name on leaderboards
  • Serve advertisements within the app
  • Improve game performance and fix bugs

4. Third-Party Services

ComBoom uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Games Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Apple Game Center

5. Data Storage and Security

Game progress and high scores are stored locally on your device. Leaderboard scores are stored by Google Play Games Services or Apple Game Center on their respective servers. We do not operate our own servers and do not store any personal data on external databases.

6. Children's Privacy

ComBoom does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require any personal data to play. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information through a third-party service, please contact the respective service provider.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Disconnect Google Play Games or Game Center from your device settings at any time
  • Reset your advertising identifier through your device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings
  • Request deletion of your leaderboard data by contacting us

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
